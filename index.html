What is Water Damage Restoration?

Water damage restoration is the process of removing excess water, drying affected areas, cleaning, sanitizing, and repairing a property after flooding, leaks, or burst pipes. The goal is to restore the space to a safe, dry condition while preventing mold, structural damage, and health risks.
