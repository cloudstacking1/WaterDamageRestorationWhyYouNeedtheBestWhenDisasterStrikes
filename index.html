What is Flood Restoration?

Flood Restoration is the professional process of cleaning, repairing, and restoring a property after it has been damaged by flooding. It involves more than just removing standing water — it includes drying out the structure, dehumidifying hidden moisture, cleaning and disinfecting affected areas (since floodwater is often contaminated), preventing mold growth, and repairing or replacing damaged materials such as flooring, drywall, and insulation.
